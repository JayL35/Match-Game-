China's Mythical Creature Matching Game

"Unique in their appearance, character and symbolic significance, the mythical creatures of Chinese folklore" are very interesting. They were thought to possess magical powers and were associated with prosperity, disaster or, in some cases, the birth of sages." Most importantly, all of these mythical creatures hold an fascinating story behind it. So now let's play a matching game to see if you would be able to identify the identical mythical creatures and if possible interest you to learn about their stories. Also, see if you would be able to find and match the one mythical creature that does not have Chinese origin in it. Good Luck!
